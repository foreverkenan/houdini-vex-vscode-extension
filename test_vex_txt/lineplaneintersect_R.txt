= lineplaneintersect_R =

#type: vex
#context: all
#tags: intersect
#group: math

"""Computes the intersection of a line and an infinite 3D plane."""

:usage: `void lineplaneintersect_R(vector line_pos0, vector line_pos1, vector plane_pos, vector plane_normal, vector &intersect_pos)`
:usage: `void lineplaneintersect_R(vector line_pos0, vector line_pos1, vector plane_pos0, vector plane_pos1, vector plane_pos2, vector &intersect_pos)`

Use the position and normal of any point on the surface (use 1 pos + 1 nor)
Or use the position of three points (use 3 pos)
